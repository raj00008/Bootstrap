DC Ambierod

The Ambierod is among the recent editions spruced up by Dilip Chhabria. The car exudes passion and ingenuity. It exhibits a perfect culmination of comfort, luxury with stunning appeal while being a state of the art car. The Ambierod is celebrated as the most strikingly redesigned retro ultramodern vehicle.

This ingenious car is influenced by the renowned Ambassador. The idea behind selecting the Ambassador design was to create a nostalgic sense associated with the iconic car while feeding the needs of the future Indian car.

DC Avanti

The car's frame is made of composite high steel and the body consists of carbon composite, to create a strong, yet lightweight vehicle.

Interior look; The interior was designed primarily with leather which is used primarily on the doors, dashboard and seats. The instrument cluster, steering wheel, and AC are all included in basic units.

Exterior look;The car is available in red, white, silver, blue, orange, grey or yellow, with black stripes. The headlamps are bi-xenon units with LED DRLS shaped like eyebrows.The vehicle also has a long low bonnet and DC logo.
